This contract allows users to register chains and bridges, and then use the `crossChain` function to transfer tokens between registered chains. The `registerChain` and `registerBridge` functions are only callable by the contract owner, and they ensure that only registered chains and bridges can be used in the `crossChain` function.

The `crossChain` function is optimized for minimal gas usage by only checking for the existence of the from and to chains and bridges, and not storing any additional information. This allows for fast and efficient cross-chain token transfers.
